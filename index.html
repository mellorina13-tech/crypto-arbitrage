What is Crypto Arbitrage?

Crypto arbitrage is a trading strategy that exploits price differences of the same cryptocurrency across different exchanges. Our free crypto arbitrage scanner monitors real-time prices from 8 major cryptocurrency exchanges including Binance, KuCoin, Gate.io, MEXC, Bybit, OKX, Huobi, and Bitget to help traders identify profitable opportunities instantly.

With our arbitrage scanner, you can track up to 250 cryptocurrencies simultaneously and discover price differences that could lead to profitable trades. The tool calculates net profits after accounting for trading fees on each exchange, giving you accurate profit estimations.
